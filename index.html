How do I start a chat? ▼
Tap the ✏️ icon at the top of the chat list or go to Menu → New Chat. Search for a user by phone number or name and tap to start chatting.
How do I create a group? ▼
Go to Menu → New Group. Enter a group name, search for members by phone, add them, then tap Create.
How does invite link work? ▼
Go to Menu → Invite Friends to get your personal invite link. Share it with anyone — when they open it and register, they can message you directly.
Is TeleChat free? ▼
Yes! TeleChat is completely free to use. Standard SMS rates may apply for the OTP verification sent by your carrier.
How do I delete a message? ▼
Long-press (or right-click on desktop) any message you sent to open the context menu, then tap Delete.
How do I log out? ▼
Go to Menu (☰) → Log Out. Your messages are saved and you can log back in anytime with the same phone number.
